Nepal's Major Festivals

Joy, Color, and Culture in Every Celebration

Dashain Tihar Holi

Dashain

Dashain is Nepal's longest and most cherished festival, celebrating the triumph of good over evil. Families reunite, tika and jamara are placed on foreheads, and blessings flow from elders. The skies fill with kites and joy permeates every home.

Tihar

Known as the Festival of Lights, Tihar honors animals, wealth, and sibling love across five vibrant days. Rangolis decorate homes, oil lamps light the night, and the air is filled with laughter and Deusi-Bhailo songs.

Holi

Holi, the Festival of Colors, bursts with joy as people splash colors, dance in the streets, and celebrate the arrival of spring. It's a playful, unforgettable day shared by all with pure happiness and love.
